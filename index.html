1. Where does come from the name of age Hadean?

The name "Hadean" comes from the Greek word "Hades," which referred to the Greek underworld because it is related to a stage of heat and confusion.

2. What was the Earth's atmosphere mainly composed of?

Earth's atmosphere consists primarily of carbon dioxide, water vapor, methane, and ammonia.

2. How did the earth obtain a primitive atmosphere?

Thanks to the surface temperatures and constant volcanic eruptions, a large amount of gases began to be expelled to the surface, accumulating for millions of years, resulting in a primitive atmosphere.
